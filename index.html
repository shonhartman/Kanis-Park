Kanis History

DIY 7 years. Collaboration between local skaters and City of Little Rock Parks and Recreation. The City buys the materials and the skaters do the work.

The Kanis Bowl was built in 1987. Some guys took it upon themselves to expand the park shaping the concrete into something cool to skate. The City took notice of the positive effect it was making on the park and helped with materials creating something unique that will last a long time. Its a nice way to work hand in hand with the City having people out here who really love the park and know what they are doing and City Staff providing support. One of the only reviews on TripAdvisor said "Nothing but hoodlums, must have 911 on speed dial". Thanks to the dedicated team of volunteers and the City's help. Kanis is turning into a very family friendly environment.

Legendary Kanis Bash

Kanis Bash is an annual fundraiser for Little Rock's Kanis Skate Park, featuring live music, art, merch, skateboarders and food. It started six years ago when finances became tight for a group of locals that had been volunteering their own money, time and services to maintain the free DIY community skate park.
